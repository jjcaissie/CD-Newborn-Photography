Hi, I’m Candace — a lover of slow mornings, good books, and meaningful connections. I’m someone who finds joy in the little things and deeply values the beauty of everyday moments.

For the past 3.5 years, I’ve worked as a postpartum nurse — supporting families through some of the most meaningful (and tender) days of their lives. While my career has recently expanded beyond just bedside nursing, I found myself missing those early newborn moments. That’s what led me here: combining my love for babies, storytelling, and quiet connection through photography.

Newborn photography allows me to honor this special season in a different way — by helping you preserve the tiny details, soft snuggles, and newness of life at home.
